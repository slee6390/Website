Project 1: Hansel and Gretel
A text-based create your own adventure style game that was inspired from the Hansel and Gretel fairytale. The player will be able to make different choices as the witch to affect the outcome of the game.

Project 2: Theory of Mind
An attempt to digitize experiments designed to test a psychological theory called Theory of Mind, which refers to the human ability to ascribe mental states to those other than ourselves and how we use such ability to understand others.
